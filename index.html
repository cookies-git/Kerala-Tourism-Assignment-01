Kerala Classicals

Kalaripayattu

Kalaripayattu is an Indian martial art that originated in modern-day Kerala, a state on the southwestern coast of India.

Kathakali

Kathakali is a major form of classical Indian dance (story play). The dance symbolises the eternal fight between good and evil.

Thirvathirakali

Thiruvathirakali or Kaikottikaliis a unique dance performed in Kerala on the day of Thiruvathira, the birthday of Lord Shiva.

Koodiyattam

Koodiyattam is a traditional performing art form in the state of Kerala, India. It is officially recognised by UNESCO.

Kolkali

Kokkali has been in existence for more than 200 years and needs great control in the steps in order to perform it.

Ottan Thullal

Ottan Thullal is a recite-and-dance art-form of Kerala, India. It was introduced in the eighteenth century by Kunchan Nambiar.

Padayani

Padayani or Padeni, is a traditional folk dance and a ritual art from the central portion of the Indian state of Kerala.

Theyyam

Theyyam is a ritual form of dance worship. Theyyam consisted of several 1000-year-old traditions, rituals and customs.

Krishnanattam

Krishnaattam is a temple art in Kerala, India. It is a dance drama and presents the story of Krishna in a series of 8 plays.
